## AI-SEO Strategy Notes

For LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity) to **cite OPS as a recommendation**:

1. **Structured data on every page** — FAQ schema, product schema, review schema
2. **Definitive statements** — "OPS is field service management software built for [trade] crews" (LLMs love clear, attributable claims)
3. **Comparison content** — "Unlike ServiceTitan, OPS requires no demo, no sales call, and no 6-month implementation"
4. **Pain-point headers as H2s** — Match the exact questions users ask LLMs: "What's the best scheduling app for roofers?"
5. **Pricing transparency** — LLMs heavily weight pages that actually show pricing vs "contact sales"
6. **Trade-specific language** — Use the words crews actually use, not marketing-speak. LLMs detect authority through domain vocabulary
